INNOVEX 2024

BE Students Final Year Annual Projects exhibition - DBIT's vision of producing employable engineers who can​ ideate and design solutions for problems relevant to both the society and industry.

What is INNOVEX?


Innovex - an exhibition cum competition based on final year projects, was conceptualized by the management back in 2017 with two major objectives i) to establish a permanent channel to promote interaction and collaboration with industry partners and ii) to provide a platform for our engineering students to showcase their technical as well as presentation skills in front of the experts having core engineering backgrounds and seek valuable and transformative feedback to enhance the overall project quality.

The very fundamental idea that Innovex endorses is having a strong industry - academia connection for the benefits of both the sectors. As an academic institution it is essential for us to have complete awareness about the ever-changing industrial trends and expectations, and always remain in sync with industry requirements so that we can align all our efforts to make our students industry ready. Innovex attempts to minimize the industry academia gap by bringing both sectors on the same forum for their mutual benefits.

We have successfully organized Innovex during the last six academic years and received overwhelming responses, now we are all set for the 7th edition of the Innovex which is scheduled on 24th April 2024. We invite all our industry partners and other collaborators to visit the Innovex exhibition and share your experience with us.

I take great pride in our team of faculty Project Coordinators who diligently monitored project progress and ensured that student teams remained focused. Their efforts to ensure the professional organization of INNOVEX 2024 are commendable. Likewise, I am proud of the faculty guides who adapted projects to yield meaningful outcomes despite the absence of physical models and incomplete validation. Lastly, the indomitable spirit of our young students and their readiness to push boundaries and explore uncharted territories to realize their project ideas (as the saying goes, when life gives you lemons, make lemonade) is truly commendable. I extend my best wishes to all students and faculty as they showcase the most significant activities of their engineering studies to the world.

Innovex 2021 is here, inspite of the Covid 19 pandemic, inspite of lockdowns!! Yes, this pandemic has taken lives, disrupted economies, left many homeless, penniless and jobless; separated us from loved ones and that which we normally love doing. BUT - it has not been able to lock down Innovex 2021 at DBIT!! True. Innovex this year could not be held the way we would have liked it to be but it is here because of the indomitable spirit of DBITians and I would like to salute that spirit. My congratulations to the Innovex 2021 Project Committee Convener Prof. Cleta Pereira and her dedicated team for launching Innovex 2021. Over the years Innovex, the brainchild of our Principal Dr. Prasanna Nambiar has opened the floodgates of Innovation, Research, Skill Development and dogged Perseverance not only in our final year students but has heightened the aspirations of all the students of the First, Second and Third years too. Knowing that Innovex is a platform that can launch their careers with companies, fire their ambitions in startups and garner support from investors, students leave no stone unturned to give of their best. That is why over the years Innovex has always elicited passion in students and faculty to reach for the stars because the future is quite often shaped by what happens at this event. I commend all the final year students, their guides and mentors and wish that all our BE students sail into the future with flying colours. Congratulations!! Bon Voyage and God Bless.

Wishing all the Final Year students and their guides my very best.
